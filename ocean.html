World Oceans

Details and Interesting Facts

Pacific Ocean

Area: 168,723,000 km²

Average Depth: 4,280 m

Deepest Point: Mariana Trench (~10,994 m)

Facts: Largest ocean, contains the Ring of Fire, has many islands including Polynesia and Micronesia.

Atlantic Ocean

Area: 85,133,000 km²

Average Depth: 3,646 m

Deepest Point: Puerto Rico Trench (~8,376 m)

Facts: Second-largest ocean, home to the Sargasso Sea, Gulf Stream affects Europe's climate.

Indian Ocean

Area: 70,560,000 km²

Average Depth: 3,741 m

Deepest Point: Java Trench (~7,258 m)

Facts: Warmest ocean, important for monsoon winds and historic trade, rich in biodiversity.

Southern Ocean

Area: 21,960,000 km²

Average Depth: 3,270 m

Deepest Point: South Sandwich Trench (~7,235 m)

Facts: Encircles Antarctica, strong currents like Antarctic Circumpolar Current, rich feeding grounds for marine life.

Arctic Ocean

Area: 15,558,000 km²

Average Depth: 1,205 m

Deepest Point: Eurasian Basin (~5,450 m)

Facts: Smallest ocean, mostly covered by sea ice, unique ecosystems with polar bears, walruses, and seals.
