Famous Rivers of the World

Nile River

Length: ~6650 km

Origin: Lake Victoria Region & Lake Tana

Flows Through: Uganda, Sudan, Egypt → Mediterranean Sea

Famous For: World's longest river; cradle of Egyptian civilization.

Amazon River

Length: ~6400 km

Origin: Andes Mountains, Peru

Flows Through: Peru, Colombia, Brazil → Atlantic Ocean

Famous For: Largest river by volume; Amazon rainforest.

Yangtze River

Length: ~6300 km

Origin: Tibetan Plateau

Flows Through: Entire China → East China Sea

Famous For: Longest river in Asia; Three Gorges Dam.

Largest Rivers of India

Ganga (Ganges)

Length: 2525 km

Origin: Gangotri Glacier, Uttarakhand

Flows Through: India & Bangladesh → Bay of Bengal

Famous For: National river of India; massive Sundarbans delta.

Indus River

Length: 3180 km

Origin: Near Lake Manasarovar, Tibetan Plateau

Flows Through: China → India (Ladakh) → Pakistan → Arabian Sea

Famous For: River of the Indus Valley Civilization.

Brahmaputra River

Length: 3848 km

Origin: Angsi Glacier, Tibet

Flows Through: Tibet → Arunachal Pradesh → Assam → Bangladesh

Famous For: Creates the world's largest river island (Majuli).
